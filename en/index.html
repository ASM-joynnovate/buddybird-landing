How it works

Sound input

FAQ

Have a question?

Which parrot species are supported? {{ faqSign0 }}

We tailor the curriculum to each bird's species, age, sex, and other traits, including conures.
Can I do this with just my phone? {{ faqSign1 }}

Yes, all you need is your smartphone. Just install the app and set it near the cage. The phone's microphone listens to your parrot and handles the training and care. You don't need any extra devices or equipment.
Does training work even when I'm away? {{ faqSign2 }}

Yes. Even when you're not there, AI repeats words for your parrot and encourages them to say it back. It listens through the mic, praises them when they get it right, and drops back to easier words when they struggle, all on its own.
How much does it cost? {{ faqSign4 }}

Word training will be a monthly subscription. We'll announce exact pricing at launch.
